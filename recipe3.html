Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions.
  2. In a separate pan, cook the ground beef until browned.
  3. Add tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
